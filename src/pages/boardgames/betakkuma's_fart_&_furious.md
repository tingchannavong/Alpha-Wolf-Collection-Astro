# Betakkuma's Fart & Furious

 Ding-Dong!  The elevator door opens as the bell goes off. Everyone hastily steps into the cabin. It is just another boring morning where all of you have to cramp yourselves into the same old elevator to go up to your office. The very moment the door shuts tight, a rumbling sound breaks the silence. The atmosphere suddenly intensifies. A foul smell immediately hits you in the face. It is as simple as that - an eye for an eye, a toot for a toot!  Fart &amp; Furious is a putridly-amusing party game for 3-8 players where you and your friends will fare against each other in a battle of PFFT!   description from the publisher
